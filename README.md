# Endless Survivors (エンドレスサバイバーズ)

A browser-based survival game where you control a character that automatically attacks enemies while you focus on movement and survival.

## Game Features

- **Mouse-controlled movement**: Your character follows your mouse cursor
- **Automatic combat**: Character automatically fires projectiles at the nearest enemy
- **Experience system**: Collect XP gems to level up
- **Endless gameplay**: Survive as long as possible against waves of enemies
- **Japanese/English UI**: Bilingual interface with Japanese fonts

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click "ゲーム開始 (Start Game)" to begin
3. Move your mouse to control your character
4. Survive as long as possible!

### Controls
- **移動 (Move)**: マウスカーソル (Mouse Cursor)
- **攻撃 (Attack)**: 自動 (Automatic)
- **目的 (Objective)**: できるだけ長く生き残る (Survive as long as possible)

## Game Mechanics

- Enemies spawn from the edges of the screen and move toward you
- Your character automatically fires at the closest enemy
- Defeating enemies drops XP gems
- Collecting XP gems increases your level
- Contact with enemies damages your health
- Game ends when your health reaches zero
